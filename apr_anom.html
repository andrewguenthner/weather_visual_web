Highlights ...

  • It was Europe's turn to bask in some warmth whiel much of North America stayed cold
  • Warmer and drier than normal weather continued in Australia, with South Australia and New South Wales having their warmest April on record -- the warm and dry conditions brought quite a bit more sunshine than normal as well
  • Sea ice extent was low in Antarctica, contributing to signfiicantly warmer than normal temperatures around the edges of the continent
